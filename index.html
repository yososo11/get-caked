Teddy Trio Collection

Meet our Teddy Trio Collection — three handcrafted bear-themed cakes, each with its own signature profile: Black Forest, Strawberry, and Chocolate. Designed with creativity, baked with love, and crafted with irresistible flavours.

Black Forest Dream

Luxurious layers of moist chocolate sponge filled with tart cherries and fresh whipped cream. A rich, nostalgic favorite made with real ingredients and finished with decadent chocolate shavings — perfect for any celebration.

Strawberry Bliss

Light layers of soft vanilla sponge filled with bright strawberry compote and whipped cream. Made with real berries for a naturally sweet, refreshing taste and a smooth cream finish — perfect for fans of fresh, fruity desserts.

Chocolate Lava Love

Rich layers of decadent chocolate sponge filled with smooth chocolate cream and rich cocoa notes. A timeless favorite made with premium ingredients for a deep, velvety flavor — perfect for every chocolate lover’s celebration.

Our Story

Get Caked was created with one goal: to turn your ideas into edible artistry. Every cake starts with inspiration from a shape, a character, a colour, a feeling — and becomes a handcrafted creation you can see, feel, and taste. We present our cakes in a more immersive way, allowing users to appreciate every detail. We believe dessert should be an experience, not just treat.
